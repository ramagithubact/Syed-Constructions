Overview

Category A Structural works involve the primary load-bearing components of a building or structure. At Syed Constructions, we focus on robust foundation, precise masonry, lintel beams, and RCC slabs to ensure the strength and stability of every structure we build.

Key Structural Activities

1. Brick Masonry

We lay bricks in cement mortar (1:6 or 1:4 ratio) with uniform joints and alignment. We use string lines, spirit levels, and plumb bobs for vertical and horizontal precision. Curing is done daily to achieve optimal strength.

2. Slab Concrete Work

We cast RCC slabs using approved mix designs (M20â€“M25) with reinforcement per structural drawings. Proper formwork, leveling, vibrator compaction, and curing ensure crack-free and level finishes.

3. Lintel Casting

Lintels are cast above doors and windows to distribute loads. We install shuttering, reinforcement bars, and cast concrete with proper curing and deshuttering timelines as per site conditions.

4. Column & Beam Framework

Columns and beams are critical for RCC framing. We ensure bar fixing, shuttering, verticality checks, and pour sequencing. Concrete is vibrated thoroughly and cured continuously for 7â€“14 days.

5. Curing and Quality Checks

Curing is done through ponding, wet cloth, or chemical sprays. Cube samples are taken for compression testing. Verticality, plumb, and level checks are performed by site engineers before proceeding to the next phase.

Tools & Team
