Smooth, Durable Finishing for Interior and Exterior Walls with Waterproofing Solutions

Overview

Plastering enhances the aesthetics and protects the structural surfaces from weather and moisture. Syed Constructions provides high-quality internal and external plastering using proven methods and materials that ensure a long-lasting, crack-free, and uniform surface.

Plastering Services We Offer

1. Internal Wall Plastering

We use cement mortar (1:5 or 1:6 ratio) to plaster internal walls. Surfaces are cleaned, roughened, and joints raked before plastering. Finished surface is smooth and plumbed accurately for further painting or tiling.

2. External Wall Plastering

External walls are plastered with waterproof additives mixed in cement mortar to resist rain and weathering. A double coat may be applied where needed, followed by curing for at least 7â€“10 days.

3. Mesh Joints for Crack Prevention

We install fiberglass mesh or GI mesh at brick and column joints, beam junctions, and conduit crossings to prevent hairline cracks. This reinforcement ensures plaster integrity over time.

4. Ceiling Plastering

We plaster ceiling surfaces neatly with proper scaffolding and finishing tools. Vertical and horizontal alignments are carefully maintained.

5. Waterproofing in Wet Areas

Bathrooms, terraces, and water tanks are plastered using chemical waterproofing compounds like Dr. Fixit or Fosroc. Slope is provided for proper drainage.

6. Surface Finishing

Final finishing includes sponge or float finish based on client preference. All corners, grooves, and arches are finished with precision using PVC beads and trowels.

Materials and Crew

We use PPC/OPC cement, sieved sand, mesh rolls, waterproofing chemicals, aluminum levels, and floats. Our masons are trained in proper curing, leveling, and alignment techniques for professional finishing.
