Expert Installation of Doors, Windows, Grills, and Custom Metal Fixtures

Overview

Fixtures mark the final touch in any construction project. At Syed Constructions, we specialize in installing high-quality doors, windows, ventilation grills, and fabricated metal frames for both security and aesthetics. Each fitting is done with precision to ensure long life, strength, and design harmony.

Fixture Services We Offer

1. Door Installation

We fix wooden, flush, laminated, or PVC doors for rooms, bathrooms, and main entrances. Frames are aligned vertically with wedges and plumb lines. Hinges, locks, and handles are installed smoothly.

2. Window Fittings

Aluminum, UPVC, and steel windows are fitted with proper alignment, sealing, and movement checks. We use anchor fasteners and silicon seals to prevent air/water leakage.

3. Fabricated MS Frames

Fabricated mild steel door and window frames are painted with red oxide and grouted during brickwork or fixed with anchor bolts. They provide strong support and durability in public and industrial buildings.

4. Ventilation & Safety Grills

We fix safety grills for windows, balconies, and ventilators using customized steel or GI bars. Aesthetic designs and weld quality are maintained for secure installation.

5. Miscellaneous Metal Fixtures

We fabricate and install ramps, railings, steel door shutters, staircase handrails, tank covers, and more as per site demands. All welding, cutting, and installation is handled by trained technicians.

Materials & Crew

Materials used include teak wood, plywood, laminated boards, MS square pipes, grills, GI hinges, handles, tower bolts, and red oxide primer. Our fabrication and carpentry teams work under direct supervision of site engineers.
