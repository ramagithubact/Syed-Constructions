PCC Work (Plain Cement Concrete)

Strong, Durable Foundations and Floor Bases with Quality Concrete Mixes

Overview

Plain Cement Concrete (PCC) is an essential stage of construction that provides a clean, level surface and strong base for further structural elements like footings, slabs, and flooring. At Syed Constructions, we deliver precision PCC work tailored to design specs, ensuring long-term durability and structural integrity.

PCC Work Execution Process

1. Site Preparation

Area is cleaned, leveled, and moistened. Formwork is set up with accurate dimensions and alignment. Antitermite treatment may be applied as needed.

2. Mixing and Ratio Control

Concrete is mixed in proper proportions (1:3:6 or 1:4:8 â€“ cement:sand:aggregate) as per project specification. We use mechanical mixers and batch measurement for uniformity.

3. Pouring and Leveling

Fresh concrete is poured inside shuttered panels and spread evenly. Wooden/metal screeds are used to achieve a level surface and specified thickness (commonly 100â€“150 mm).

4. Compaction

Concrete is compacted manually or using plate vibrators to eliminate air gaps and improve density. Attention is given to corners and joints to avoid honeycombing.

5. Curing

We cure the surface with water for a minimum of 7 days using ponding or wet hessian cloths. Proper curing is key to avoid shrinkage cracks and to gain full strength.

6. Applications

PCC is used under footings, columns, flooring slabs, external paving, compound walls, and drainage channels. It ensures a clean work base and controls soil moisture below RCC structures.

Materials & Manpower

We use quality cement (53 grade), screened river sand, machine-crushed blue metal, and potable water. Our skilled laborers follow instructions from site supervisors and engineers to execute clean and accurate PCC layers.
